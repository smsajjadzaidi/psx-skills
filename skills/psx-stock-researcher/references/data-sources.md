# PSX Data Sources Reference

A guide to what each data source provides and when to use it.

## Primary Fundamental Sources

### 1. Pakistan Stock Exchange Official Portal
**URL Pattern:** `https://dps.psx.com.pk/company/[TICKER]`
**Announcements:** `https://dps.psx.com.pk/company/[TICKER]/announcements`
**Provides:**
- Official company profile and sector classification
- Current price, volume, market cap
- Financial statements (income statement, balance sheet)
- Corporate announcements (dividends, rights issues, AGM notices)
- Historical price data
- Index membership (KSE-100, KMI-30, KMI All Share etc.)

**Best for:** KMI index membership, official announcements, financial statements, recent disclosures

---

### 2. Sarmaaya
**URL Pattern:** `https://sarmaaya.pk/psx/company/[TICKER]`
**Also check:** `https://sarmaaya.pk/stocks/[TICKER]` for editorial/analyst content
**Provides:**
- Shariah compliance status (explicitly stated)
- Key ratios: P/E, P/B, EPS, dividend yield
- Dividend history with dates and amounts
- Technical indicators and moving averages
- Analyst commentary (public articles and YouTube content)

**Best for:** Shariah compliance verification, dividend history, community analyst coverage

---

### 3. Stock Analysis (PSX)
**URL Pattern:**
- Overview: `https://stockanalysis.com/quote/psx/[TICKER]/`
- Financials: `https://stockanalysis.com/quote/psx/[TICKER]/financials/`
- Statistics: `https://stockanalysis.com/quote/psx/[TICKER]/statistics/`
- Revenue: `https://stockanalysis.com/quote/psx/[TICKER]/revenue/`

**Provides:**
- Multi-year revenue and earnings trends
- Quarterly breakdowns
- Comprehensive ratio table (ROE, ROIC, current ratio, debt/equity)
- Free cash flow data
- Beta, market cap, shares outstanding

**Best for:** Trend analysis, multi-year financials, FCF, ROIC

---

### 4. SCS Trade Snapshot
**URL Pattern:** `https://scstrade.com/stockscreening/SS_CompanySnapShot.aspx?symbol=[TICKER]`
**Provides:**
- Quick ratio snapshot
- Gross margin, sales growth QoQ and YoY
- Book value per share
- Enterprise value per share

**Best for:** Quick margin and growth cross-check

---

### 5. Investing.com
**URL Pattern:** Search `[Company Name] Pakistan investing.com`
**Provides:**
- Technical analysis summary (buy/sell signals)
- 52-week high/low
- Analyst ratings where available

**Best for:** Technical sentiment, 52-week range confirmation

---

## News Sources (Ranked by PSX Relevance)

### 6. Business Recorder — PRIMARY news source
**Search:** `"[TICKER]" site:brecorder.com 2026`
**URL:** `https://brecorder.com`
**Why:** Pakistan's most PSX-focused financial newspaper. Brokers publish research summaries here. Covers earnings, corporate actions, sector news daily.
**Best for:** Company news, broker upgrade/downgrade summaries, SECP notices, quarterly result commentary

---

### 7. Profit by Pakistan Today
**Search:** `"[TICKER]" site:profit.com.pk 2026`
**URL:** `https://profit.pakistantoday.com.pk`
**Why:** Best long-form English PSX coverage. Readable sector deep dives, IPO analysis, corporate breakdowns.
**Best for:** Sector analysis, company narratives, readable corporate breakdowns

---

### 8. Mettis Global
**Search:** `"[TICKER]" site:mettis.com 2026`
**URL:** `https://mettis.com`
**Why:** PSX-focused financial data and news. Covers broker reports, financial results, market data.
**Best for:** Broker report coverage, financial result filings

---

### 9. Dawn Business
**Search:** `"[TICKER]" site:dawn.com 2026`
**URL:** `https://www.dawn.com/business`
**Why:** Quality macro and policy journalism. Best for SBP decisions, IMF developments, budget/fiscal policy.
**Best for:** Macro environment, regulatory changes, government policy affecting sectors

---

### 10. The News Business
**Search:** `"[TICKER]" site:thenews.com.pk 2026`
**URL:** `https://www.thenews.com.pk/category/business`
**Best for:** Corporate results, market commentary, supplementary coverage

## Macro Data Sources

### 11. State Bank of Pakistan (SBP)
**URL:** `https://www.sbp.org.pk`
**Key pages:**
- Monetary policy / policy rate: `https://www.sbp.org.pk/monpolicy/index.asp`
- Foreign exchange reserves: `https://www.sbp.org.pk/ecodata/index2.asp`

**Provides:** Authoritative policy rate, reserve levels, MPS statements, banking regulation
**Best for:** Confirming SBP rate, reserve figures, monetary policy direction

---

### 12. Pakistan Bureau of Statistics (PBS)
**URL:** `https://www.pbs.gov.pk`
**Provides:** CPI inflation (monthly), LSM industrial output index, GDP data
**Best for:** Official inflation numbers, industrial activity proxy

## Standard Macro Search Queries

Run the general queries + the sector-specific one for the stock being researched:

**General (always run):**
| What you need | Search query |
|--------------|--------------|
| SBP policy rate | `SBP policy rate decision Pakistan 2026` |
| IMF program | `IMF Pakistan program tranche review 2026` |
| PKR/USD | `PKR USD exchange rate Pakistan 2026` |
| CPI inflation | `Pakistan CPI inflation 2026` |
| Foreign reserves | `Pakistan foreign exchange reserves SBP 2026` |

**Sector-specific (run the matching one):**
| Sector | Query |

## KMI Index Verification

To verify Shariah compliance:
1. Check KMI-30 or KMI All Share Index membership on PSX official portal
2. Sarmaaya's explicit compliance label
3. If conflicting, default to PSX official KMI index list as authoritative

KMI index composition reviewed semi-annually. Always note date of last verification.

## Common Ticker Formats

PSX tickers are typically 3-6 uppercase letters:
- Banks: MEBL, HBL, UBL, MCB, ABL, BAHL
- Energy/E&P: OGDC, PPL, MARI, POL
- Fertilizer: FFC, EFERT, FFBL
- Cement: LUCK, DGKC, MLCF, PIOC, CHCC
- Technology: SYS, TRG, NETSOL, PAEL
- Auto: SAZEW, HCAR, INDU, PSMC
- Pharma: SEARL, HALEON, ILP, NPL, HINOON
- REITs: DCR, AREIT, SREIT
- ETFs: MZNPETF, NFSETF
