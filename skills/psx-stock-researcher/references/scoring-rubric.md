# PSX Stock Scoring Rubric

Use this rubric to calibrate verdicts consistently across different stocks and sectors.

## Verdict Definitions

| Verdict | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| **BUY** | Strong fundamentals, reasonable valuation, positive momentum, no major red flags. Suitable to initiate or add to position now. |
| **ACCUMULATE** | Good fundamentals but either valuation is slightly stretched or momentum is uncertain. Buy in tranches rather than all at once. |
| **HOLD** | Fundamentals intact but limited near-term upside. Keep existing position but don't add aggressively. |
| **REDUCE** | Fundamentals weakening OR valuation stretched OR governance concerns. Consider trimming position size. |
| **AVOID** | Poor fundamentals, major governance concerns, or valuation is extreme. Not recommended for new positions. |

## Red Flag Severity Guide

### 🔴 High Severity (consider AVOID or REDUCE)
- SECP enforcement action or PSX suspension history
- Auditor qualified opinion or going concern note
- Reverse merger with shell company
- Promoter pledging >50% of shares
- Related-party transactions >20% of revenue
- Negative equity

### 🟡 Medium Severity (note and monitor)
- Auditor changed in last 2 years without explanation
- High related-party transactions (10-20% of revenue)
- Circular debt exposure (energy sector)
- Thin float / low liquidity
- Declining revenue for 2+ consecutive years

### 🟢 Low Severity (worth noting but not disqualifying)
- Single large customer concentration
- Commodity price exposure
- Currency risk (import-dependent)
- Sector regulatory changes pending

## Shariah Compliance Thresholds (KMI Standard)

A stock fails Shariah compliance if:
- Interest-bearing debt > 37% of total assets
- Non-permissible income > 5% of total revenue  
- Business activity is in a prohibited sector (alcohol, tobacco, conventional banking, weapons)
- Accounts receivable + cash > 75% of total assets (asset-light test)

## Dividend Yield Assessment

| Yield | Assessment |
|-------|-----------|
| >12% | Exceptional — verify sustainability (high yield often signals price distress) |
| 8-12% | Strong — good income play if payout is sustainable |
| 5-8% | Healthy — solid income with likely growth component |
| 2-5% | Moderate — growth stock with some income |
| <2% | Low — primarily a growth/capital gains play |
| 0% | No income — evaluate purely on growth thesis |

Always calculate after-tax yield: `After-tax yield = Stated yield × 0.85` (15% WHT in Pakistan)

## Beta Interpretation (PSX context)

| Beta | Risk Profile |
|------|-------------|
| < 0.5 | Low volatility — defensive stock |
| 0.5 - 0.8 | Below market volatility — relatively stable |
| 0.8 - 1.2 | Market-like volatility — moves with KSE-100 |
| 1.2 - 2.0 | High volatility — amplified market moves |
| > 2.0 | Very high volatility — speculative / small cap |

## Macro Impact by Sector

Cross the stock's sector with active macro factors to assess net tailwind/headwind. If 2+ headwinds active simultaneously, downgrade verdict by at least one level (BUY → ACCUMULATE, ACCUMULATE → HOLD).

| Macro Factor | Tailwind For | Headwind For |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| **SBP rate cuts** | Banks (credit demand), leveraged companies, real estate, auto | — |
| **SBP rate hikes** | Banks (NIM expansion short-term), cash-heavy defensives | Cement, auto, leveraged cos, REITs |
| **PKR depreciation** | Exporters: textiles, IT/tech, E&P (USD revenues) | Importers: auto (CKD), pharma (APIs), FMCG (raw materials) |
| **PKR appreciation** | Importers: auto, pharma, FMCG | Exporters: textiles, tech |
| **High/rising inflation** | Pass-through cos (FMCG leaders), commodity producers | Margin-sensitive manufacturers, consumer discretionary |
| **IMF program active** | PSX broadly, banks, currency-sensitive stocks | — |
| **IMF program stalled** | Defensives, USD-denominated assets | Banks, cyclicals, PSX broadly |
| **Oil price rise** | OGDC, PPL, MARI, POL (E&P revenues) | Fertilizer (gas feedstock), IPPs (fuel cost) |
| **Oil price fall** | Fertilizer, IPPs (gas cost), consumer-facing | E&P sector |
| **High circular debt** | — | IPPs (delayed capacity payments), E&P (receivables buildup) |
| **PSDP spending up** | Cement, steel, construction | — |
| **Rising PIB yields** | Banks (investment book, NIM long-term) | REITs, high-dividend plays (opportunity cost rises) |
| **Falling PIB yields** | REITs, dividend plays, leveraged cos | Banks (NIM compression) |

## Investor Profile Matching

When assessing portfolio fit, match stocks to investor profiles:

**Conservative investor:** Beta <0.8, dividend yield >6%, established company, KSE-100 constituent, no governance flags
**Moderate investor:** Beta 0.8-1.5, mix of dividend and growth, reasonable P/E vs sector, KMI compliant preferred
**Aggressive investor:** Higher beta acceptable, growth over income, smaller caps, emerging sectors (tech, renewable energy)
